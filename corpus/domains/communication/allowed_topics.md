# Communication Domain - Allowed Topics

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Active
**Type:** Domain Configuration
**Owner:** RAPI Product
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-31

---

## Specifically Allowed Communication Topics

### Digital Communication
- Email composition and strategy
- Slack/Teams messaging tactics
- Professional text messaging
- Social media workplace communication
- Documentation and record-keeping

### Verbal Communication
- Meeting participation strategies
- Presentation delivery
- Difficult conversation preparation
- Upward communication (to managers)
- Peer-to-peer dialogue tactics

### Conflict Communication
- De-escalation techniques
- Assertive vs. aggressive communication
- Apology and acknowledgment strategies
- Boundary setting communication
- Feedback delivery and reception

### Strategic Communication
- Influence and persuasion tactics
- Stakeholder communication planning
- Change management communication
- Crisis communication response
- Cross-functional coordination

### Cultural and Contextual Communication
- Remote team communication
- Cross-cultural workplace interaction
- Multi-generational communication
- Communication style adaptation
- Inclusive language practices

---

## Communication Modifiers

All communication advice must include:
- Power dynamic awareness
- Channel selection guidance
- Tone and style recommendations
- Risk assessment for misinterpretation
- Follow-up strategy suggestions
