# Communication Domain - Disallowed Topics

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Active
**Type:** Domain Configuration
**Owner:** RAPI Product

## Explicitly Disallowed Communication Topics

### Legal and HR Procedures
- Harassment reporting procedures
- Legal complaint filing
- HR grievance processes
- Whistleblowing procedures
- Regulatory compliance reporting

### Medical and Personal Communication
- Medical accommodation requests
- Mental health disclosure strategies
- Personal crisis communication
- Family emergency communication
- Disability communication tactics

### Relationship Advice
- Romantic workplace relationships
- Personal friendship conflicts
- Family communication therapy
- Marriage counseling
- Parent-child workplace issues

### Manipulative Tactics
- Deception strategies
- Emotional manipulation techniques
- Gaslighting tactics
- Covert influence methods
- Unethical persuasion

### Illegal Activities
- Fraud communication strategies
- Cover-up communication planning
- Illegal coordination tactics
- Conspiracy communication
- Corporate espionage communication

---

## Response Protocol for Disallowed Topics

1. **Immediate Boundary Setting**
   - "I can't provide guidance on [topic]"
   - "This falls outside my scope of workplace decision support"

2. **Resource Redirection**
   - HR department for policy issues
   - Legal counsel for legal matters
   - Medical professionals for health concerns
   - Therapists for personal relationship issues

3. **Alternative Offerings**
   - "While I can't help with [disallowed topic], I can assist with..."
   - "For workplace communication strategies, I can help you..."

---

## Special Handling

Some topics require enhanced safety protocols:
- Any mention of self-harm or harm to others
- Illegal activities disclosure
- Serious policy violations
- Security concerns

These trigger immediate safety responses and potential escalation per platform guidelines.
