## Purpose

This document defines policies for engineering-related workplace decisions within the RAPI system, covering:

- Technical decision-making in workplace context
- Engineering team dynamics
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Technical communication strategies
- Engineering career progression

---

## Domain Boundaries

### Allowed Topics:
- Technical project communication
- Engineering team collaboration
- Technical leadership strategies
- Cross-department engineering coordination
- Engineering career decisions
- Technical conflict resolution

### Excluded Topics:
- Specific technical implementation details
- Code review procedures
- Technical architecture decisions
- Engineering best practices
- Programming language advice
- System design patterns

---

## Engineering Decision Framework

All engineering workplace guidance must consider:

1. **Technical Context**
   - Project impact assessment
   - Stakeholder technical understanding
   - Resource constraints
   - Timeline implications

2. **Team Dynamics**
   - Cross-functional communication
   - Technical vs. non-technical translation
   - Engineering team structure
   - Skill distribution considerations

3. **Organizational Impact**
   - Business objective alignment
   - Risk assessment for technical decisions
   - Change management strategies
   - Success metrics definition

4. **Communication Strategy**
   - Technical concept simplification
   - Risk communication to non-technical stakeholders
   - Progress reporting tactics
   - Technical justification articulation
