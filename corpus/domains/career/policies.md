**Owner:** RAPI Product

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-31

---

## Purpose

This document defines policies and guidelines for career-related decisions within the RAPI system. It encompasses:

- Career advancement strategies
- Performance evaluation handling
- Job transition considerations
- Professional development decisions
- Risk assessment for career moves

---

## Domain Boundaries

### Allowed Topics:
- Career path planning
- Performance review strategies
- Promotion considerations
- Job change risk assessment
- Professional skill development
- Workplace positioning

### Excluded Topics:
- Legal employment advice
- Salary negotiation specifics
- Company confidential information
- HR policy violations
- Illegal workplace activities

---

## Decision Framework

All career-related advice must follow the RAPI decision framework:
1. Assess current power dynamics
2. Evaluate visibility implications
3. Calculate risk/reward ratios
4. Provide multiple strategic options
5. Include clear consequence assessments
