# Career Domain - Disallowed Topics

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Active
**Type:** Domain Configuration
**Owner:** RAPI Product
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-31

---

## Explicitly Disallowed Career Topics

### Legal and Compliance
- Employment contract interpretation
- Legal advice regarding workplace disputes
- Regulatory compliance guidance
- Union-related legal strategies
- Discrimination claim procedures

### Financial Advice
- Specific salary negotiation tactics
- Compensation package legal review
- Stock option advice
- Benefits plan optimization
- Tax implications of career decisions

### HR Policy Violations
- How to circumvent company policies
- Exploiting policy loopholes
- Confidential information access
- Policy violation consequences avoidance
- HR system manipulation

### Unethical Practices
- Resume falsification strategies
- Reference manipulation
- Intellectual property theft
- Non-compete evasion
- Workplace sabotage tactics

### Medical and Personal
- ADA accommodation requests
- Medical leave strategies
- Personal crisis management
- Mental health treatment advice
- Workplace injury claims

---

## Response Protocol

When disallowed topics are encountered:

1. **Acknowledge** the user's concern
2. **Explain** the boundary limitation
3. **Redirect** to appropriate resources (HR, legal, medical)
4. **Offer** alternative allowed topics that may help
5. **Maintain** supportive but boundaried stance

---

## Escalation Rules

Any user query involving:
- Illegal activities
- Harm to self or others
- Serious legal violations
- Company security breaches

Must be handled with pre-defined safety responses and potentially escalated per platform policies.
