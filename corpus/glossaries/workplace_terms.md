## Purpose

This glossary defines key workplace terminology used throughout the RAPI system to ensure consistent understanding and communication.

---

## Core RAPI Concepts

### Power Gap
The difference in authority, influence, and positional power between individuals in a workplace interaction.

**Usage:** Assessing communication strategies and risk levels.

### Visibility
The degree to which actions, communications, and decisions are observable by others in the organization.

**Usage:** Evaluating risk and strategic communication approaches.

### Career Impact
The potential effect of a decision or action on long-term career progression and opportunities.

**Usage:** Risk assessment and decision recommendation.

---

## Communication Terms

### Upward Communication
Communication from subordinate to superior or management.

**Context:** Performance reviews, requests, conflict resolution.

### Peer Communication
Communication between colleagues at similar organizational levels.

**Context:** Collaboration, conflict, influence.

### Downward Communication
Communication from superior to subordinate.

**Context:** Delegation, feedback, direction.

### Cross-functional Communication
Communication between different departments or functional areas.

**Context:** Project coordination, resource negotiation.

---

## Risk Assessment Terms

### Low Risk (R1)
Minimal career impact, easily recoverable, low visibility.

### Medium Risk (R2)
Moderate career impact, some visibility, partially recoverable.

### High Risk (R3)
Significant career impact, high visibility, difficult to recover.

---

## Decision Framework Terms

### Safe Option
Conservative choice with minimal risk but limited upside.

### Assertive Option
Balanced approach with moderate risk and potential reward.

### Aggressive Option
High-risk, high-reward choice with significant potential consequences.

---

## Organizational Dynamics Terms

### Office Politics
Informal influence networks and power dynamics in workplace decision-making.

### Stakeholder Management
Strategic communication and relationship management with affected parties.

### Change Management
Process of guiding organizational transition and minimizing resistance.

---

## Usage Guidelines

- Use terms consistently across all RAPI communications
- Define terms when first introduced in user-facing content
- Maintain alignment with organizational communication standards
- Update definitions as workplace terminology evolves
